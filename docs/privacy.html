PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Trail Seeker is a mobile game developed by Trail Seeker Dev. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Trail Seeker stores game progress (scan history, gear inventory, resources, streak data) locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data does not leave your device.

If you connect a Solana wallet, your public wallet address is stored locally to associate your profile. We do not store private keys or seed phrases.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, location data, contacts, or browsing history. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Services

The app may connect to the Solana blockchain network for wallet-related features. These interactions are governed by the respective network's terms. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

Trail Seeker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
