A once-a-day survival RPG with real strategy

  • Players get 4–10 Seeker Scans per day (based on streak + gear)
  • Each Scan is a risk/reward decision: Scout (safe), Seeker (balanced), or Gambit (high risk)
  • Gear builds change how you play — not just your stats
  • A 7-day streak ladder rewards consistency without punishing misses
  • One 5-minute session. Real choices. Come back tomorrow for better odds.

SKR is the game’s economy — not a bolt-on

$SKR UseCostWhat it does

Whiff Protection10 $SKRRefunds a failed Scan

Every $SKR spent is split 50/50: half burned (deflationary), half deposited into a Season Reward Pool that pays out to top players at season end.

$SKR spending is optional. F2P players progress fine on free Scans. Paying players get convenience — not a separate power tier.

Built for Day 1, Day 7, and Day 30 retention

Day 1

4 Scans, instant loot, immediate progress. The game feels complete from the first session.

Day 7

Streak bonus maxes out — 7 Scans + 15% rare chance. This is the day players feel invested.

Day 30

Pathfinder Module assembly (4 rare components) unlocks a 4th gear slot. The season-long chase.

Push notifications remind players their Scans are ready. Quiet hours respected. Streak warnings are gentle, not aggressive.
