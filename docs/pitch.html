A once-a-day survival RPG with real strategy

  • Players get 4–10 Seeker Scans per day (based on streak + gear). Each scan is a risk/reward decision.
  • 3 scan types: Scout (safe), Seeker (balanced), Gambit (high risk with a real-time skill check mini-game)
  • 6 gear slots, pick 3 — builds change how you play, not just stats. Gambit builds, grinder builds, survivor builds.
  • 7-day streak ladder: show up consecutively and your scans get sharper. Miss a day and it resets — or spend $SKR to protect it.
  • 17 gear items across 3 tiers (standard, enhanced, ultra), 2 authored maps with 14 hand-written tiles, RPS arcade minigame

$SKR spending is optional. F2P players progress fine on free Scans. Paying players get convenience — not a separate power tier.

Pathfinder Module assembly (4 rare components) unlocks a 4th gear slot. The season-long chase.
